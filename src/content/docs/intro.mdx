Nuanom is a software and education provider that empowers entrepreneurs with 
knowledge and software to build and grow their businesses. We are focused on empowering e-commerce businesses in the
African market, with initial focus on Ghana.

## Software

Nuanom is comprised of the following apps and APIs:

- **Merchant App**: This is our flagship app that allows merchants to manage their
  products, orders, and customers. It is available on [Android](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.nuanom.apps.merchant) and [iOS](https://apps.apple.com/gh/app/nuanom/id6470391999).
- **Shop Web Storefront**: This is your shop's website that allows your customers to browse products, place orders, and track their orders. Your shop website is available at `<shopslug>.nuanom.shop`. We also support custom domains.
- **White Label Shop App**: Available for premium customers. This app allows your customers to browse products, place orders, and track their orders. 
  This app is distributed on Android and iOS. We manage uptime and updates automatically.
