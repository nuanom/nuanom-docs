Good news: it practically does.

These guides walk you through everything — from setting up your shop for the first time to managing a steady flow of orders. Pick up wherever you are.

## New here?

Start with the essentials and work your way down the list:

1. [Download the Nuanom app](/guides/merchant/) and create your account
2. [Fill in your shop info](/guides/merchant/shop-info/) — logo, about, contacts
3. [Set your delivery options](/guides/merchant/delivery-options/) so customers know how to get their orders
4. [Upload your products](/guides/merchant/upload-products/) — the fun part
5. [Share your shop](/guides/merchant/sharing-links/) and start taking orders

## Already up and running?

- Spice things up → [Customize your shop](/guides/merchant/customize-shop/)
- Keep things organized → [Manage departments & categories](/guides/merchant/departments-categories/)
- Speed up fulfillment → [Process orders in batches](/guides/merchant/managing-orders/)
- Sell in person too → [Set up POS devices](/guides/merchant/manage-pos-devices/)
- Get paid → [Understand settlements](/guides/merchant/settlement/)
