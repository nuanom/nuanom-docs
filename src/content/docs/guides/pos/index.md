## Download Nuanom POS

The Nuanom POS is used to take orders in-store. You will need an active shop on the [Nuanom](/guides/merchant) app to setup and manage POS devices.

- **Android**: [Download on Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.nuanom.apps.pos)
- **iOS**: [Download on the App Store](https://apps.apple.com/gh/app/nuanom-pos/id6754317027)

## Log in with passcode

- In the [Nuanom](/guides/merchant) app go to ***Business > POS***. Create a POS with a name that works for you eg. Counter 1, Afia, etc.
- Tap on the POS you created in the Nuanom app and tap on *Log in POS*. You will be given a passcode which you use to log in to the Nuanom POS app.

Now you can start taking orders with the Nuanom POS app.
