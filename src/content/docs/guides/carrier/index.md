## Download Nuanom Carrier

The Nuanom Carrier app is used to confirm pickups and deliveries for Nuanom shops.

- **Android**: [Download on Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.nuanom.apps.merchantcarrier)
- **iOS**: [Download on the App Store](https://apps.apple.com/gh/app/nuanom-carrier/id6758279697)

## Log in with mobile number

Each carrier should use their personal or business-assigned number to log in to the Nuanom Carrier app. This ensures every individual can be identified separately.

During the first login, the carrier will have to provide their first and last name for identification.

The carrier can then start confirming pickups and deliveries for Nuanom shops.
